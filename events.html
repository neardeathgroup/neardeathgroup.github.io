2nd Annual Event April 7, 8, and 9, 2017

APRIL 7, 8 & 9TH • 2017 • WIMBERLEY, TX
PRESENTATIONS • SOCIALIZING • FOOD
Come and learn what the Near Death Experience (NDE) teaches us about living, and how this wisdom can improve your life. Come Share the Soulful Enlightenment of the Near Death and Transcendent Experiencers. The symposium will cover NDE’s and related spiritually transformative topics from philosophical and therapeutic standpoints. The lecturers are experts in the their given fields, and people that have had near death and spiritually enlightening experiences.

Enjoy the “Transcendent NDE Experience” and hear first hand how these experiences have Impacted peoples lives. Let these experiencers tell you what their NDE has meant to them and how it’s changed their view of life and death. You will be able to visit with experiencers one-on-one, during the breaks and at the social mixers and during meals. They can give you a personal perspective of their spiritually transformative occurrence, and how these new truths effect everyday life. Come and listen to their amazing stories.

 Robin Landsong – Near Death Experiencer 
RobinLandsong.com
When I was eight years old, in 1977, I was abducted by an American from the US and taken to Africa. We landed in Rhodesia during the Bush War. I was later abandoned out in the countryside and spent the night alone in the bush. In the morning I made my way to a tribal village who took me in. They became my new family. I learned how to sing their songs, how to prepare food, how to help in the garden, and I participated in their ceremonies. One woman adopted me as her own and became my Mama. Weeks later, I was shot by guerrilla soldiers and crossed over into death.On the other side I met five beings who led me through my confusion and pain to acceptance, peace, and then to profound love. In this state of pure love I looked back and saw Mama holding my body and wailing. Though I treasured her, I resumed crossing through veils on my journey home to the Great Heart. I then began to hear a song that made me pause. My mama’s wailing had turned into a calling song, reminding me of my purpose for living. I turned around and came back to life so I could become a Medicine Singer like her. Like a long stream of light coming through a high stained glass window I returned to my body.

David Drapela – Near Death Experiencer 
Meet the real life Forest Gump. David Drapela’s life is an adventure that books are written about. From being broke, to cruising the world for years on a yacht, David has seen the highest of highs, and the lowest of lows. A the age of eleven, he borrowed $25 dollars from his Mother and opened a fireworks stand and made as much as an adult man would have made in a year. His life went on like this, from one creation to another. David says that he never had an original thought, he just looked to the universe for answers and followed its guidance.  One of David’s many blessings has been his ability to write many spiritual writings. He has done this for over forty years. His favorite sayings, “Stay in the River” and “Be in the world, but not of it.” As a young child he had insights that were hard for adults to understand.

Six years ago he felt his pulse rate begin to slip, it got slower and slower until he lost consciousness. He drifted off into a different realm, where he was met by angels and an immense and overwhelming love.  David was then returned to his body with a message that if he went back to sleep, that he would not come back. Confused, and not knowing what to do when he regained consciousness, and went next door to his sister’s house, and asked her to take him to the hospital.  Once arriving, the ER Doctor said, “Nobody walks in here with a heart rate that low.”

As you join this remarkable man at the symposium, he will share insights he’s been given about life and how to live it. His loving presence is felt by everyone in the room. When he delivers his messages, it is entertaining and thought provoking.
