Accomodations

Omni Austin Hotel Southpark



1004 & 4140 Governors Row, Austin, TX 78744

https://www.omnihotels.com/hotels/austin-southpark

At the intersection of Highway 71 and Interstate 35, this modern, upscale hotel is 4.9 miles from the Texas Capitol building, and 5.5 miles from Barton Springs Pool in Zilker Park.

Polished, traditional rooms feature flat-screens and WiFi access, as well as minibars and coffeemakers. Suites add features such as separate living and dining areas, wet bars, microwaves and minifridges. Some rooms have child-friendly decor.

Amenities include an indoor/outdoor pool, a spa and fitness facilities, as well as a grill-style restaurant, a lounge and a coffee shop. There's also a business center, meeting space and children's summer activities.
